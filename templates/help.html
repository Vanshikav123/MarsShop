Help for Customers

  • I want to provide Feedback

    • ▼

    If you wish to provide feedback about a specific dish you ordered, you can do so by visiing the dish's page and submitting a review over there. If you wish to write a feedback about MarsFood as a service you can contact us at marsfood.online@gmail.com or marsfood.online@rediffmail.com.

  • Can I edit my order?

    • ▼

    Once order is placed and restaurant starts preparing your food, you can not edit its contents

  • Can I order in advance?

    • ▼

    We currently do not support this functionality. All our orders are placed and executed on-demand.

  • How long do you take to deliver?

    • ▼

    Standard delivery times vary by the location selected and prevailing conditions. Once you select your location, an estimated delivery time is mentioned for each restaurant.

  • I want to cancel my order

    • ▼

    We will do our best to accommodate your request if the order is not placed to the restaurant (Customer service number:8445050264). Please note that we will have a right to charge a cancellation fee up to full order value to compensate our restaurant and delivery partners if your order has been confirmed.

  • Do you charge for delivery?

    • ▼

    Delivery fee varies from city to city and is applicable if order value is below a certain amount. Additionally, certain restaurants might have fixed delivery fees. Delivery fee (if any) is specified on the 'Review Order' page.
